My Philosophies

Technical Leadership

Make on-call not suck.
My approach emphasizes building highly resilient systems, fostering a culture of ownership, and enabling engineers to deliver impactful results without sacrificing their personal time.

Observability

Measure everything; assume nothing.
Instrument every feasible aspect of the system with measures that allow us to tell a story of the current user experience. I don't (only) want to know where the errors are, I want to know how they are tangibly impacting our customers.

CI/CD

Friday deploys shouldn't be scary.
Teams fear Friday deploys because their pipelines are too slow and their observability isn't robust enough. The fear of Friday deploys is a leading indicator of unaddressed tech debt.

Embrace Boredom

Keep toolsets simple.
Context-switching is mentally taxing, especially during incidents. Prefer managed solutions that reduce complexity and cognitive load, even if that means giving up some customization. Avoid the allure of that F1 racer when all you really need is a Civic.
